# SATG Research Information Archive

## Purpose

This directory is the **master source of truth** for all knowledge, findings,
methodological decisions, empirical results, and analysis related to the
Structure-Aware Trust Gating (SATG) project. It feeds directly into the
research paper writing process.

Every file here is a **living document** — updated whenever new information is
learned, experiments are run, or the plan evolves.
